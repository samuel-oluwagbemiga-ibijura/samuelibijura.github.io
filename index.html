About me

I am an information security professional transitioning into Governance, Risk and Compliance, with a foundation built in security operations — log analysis, SIEM monitoring, malware investigation, and network traffic analysis at Hybrid Security Consults and Bincom Global. That background matters: I understand the threats governance frameworks are designed to stop, not just what the controls say.

My view on security is simple. The strongest posture is one that prevents incidents before they happen. That is what drew me to GRC — it is where the real prevention work lives, in the risk assessments, the policies, the controls, and the governance structures built before anything goes wrong.

Alongside formal roles and job simulations with Mastercard, Commonwealth Bank, and Tata Consultancy Services, I built a five-project GRC portfolio applied to CyroPay — a fictional UK payment fintech. The work covers a full ISO 27001:2022 gap assessment across 93 controls, ten mapped information security policies, a DORA-aligned vendor risk assessment, a GDPR DPIA for a biometric authentication feature, and a board-level risk presentation. Every deliverable was produced using real frameworks and industry tools. The work is live on GitHub.
